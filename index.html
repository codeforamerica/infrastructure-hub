Welcome!

The City of Atlanta is planning a $250 million infrastructure bond measure, and is currently seeking input from citizens. You can use this site to learn more about the proposed projects, vote on which projects are important to you, and provide feedback on what kinds of improvements you would like to see to Atlanta's pedestrian and transportation infrastructure.
